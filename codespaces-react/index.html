WithMyStar

What WithMyStar Can Do:
  • Relay your queries to multiple Google Chat destinations via secure webhooks.
  • Provide instant feedback and results for your input, with error handling and input validation.
  • Maintain a history of your queries and results for easy review.
  • Support cyberpunk-themed, accessible, and responsive UI with customizable display settings.
  • Enable gamification, evolution, and agentic logic for advanced LLM playground features.
  • Integrate with Google OAuth for authentication and role management (backend).
  • Monitor errors and resources, with compliance logging for secure operations.
